ABOUT US

Who are we?

mZee Autos understand the feeling over a wrecked car, driveable or not. ALFA Collision Center is here for you. While still staying true to our company ethics, our team goal is to minimize any hassle along the collision repair process. As a customer we would like you to always expect quality, professional, and reliable repairs; every time. We thank you for allowing us to work on your vehicle.

What is our aim?

Our aim is to provide services of preventive maintenance of motor vehicles as well as for repairs and overhauls, Preventive maintenance cuts the down time and increases, life of the vehicle a reliable and well equipped automobile workshop is a must for this purpose.

~ Manager mZee Autos
